I am a computer science graduate working in cybersecurity, focused on building, analyzing, and understanding systems from the inside out.

Most of my work is hands-on — building, breaking, and improving systems through my home lab, where I run servers, manage VMs, and experiment with real setups, automation, and security-related challenges.

This site is where I document that work:

- Home lab infrastructure and systems
- Automation and tooling
- CTF writeups focused on thinking and decision-making
- Real projects built from real problems

Most things here did not work the first time. They were tested, broken, and fixed until they did.
